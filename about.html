About Me

I am a passionate and driven B.Tech student specializing in Electronics and Telecommunication Engineering at MIT Academy of Engineering, Pune, maintaining a strong academic performance with a CGPA of 8.06.

My interests lie at the intersection of Embedded Systems, IoT, and Artificial Intelligence, where I focus on creating intelligent, efficient, and scalable hardware–software integrated solutions.

I have hands-on expertise in STM32 microcontrollers, Arduino, and MATLAB/Simulink for real-time system design and control. On the software side, I work with Python, C++, and OpenCV for image processing, automation, and AI-based detection systems.

My goal is to contribute to AI-driven embedded systems and smart automation by combining strong engineering principles with creative innovation and interdisciplinary learning.
